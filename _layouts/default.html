Patrick Naughton

I am a Research Scientist in the robotics team within Vladlen Koltun's research organization at Apple.

Before that I was a PhD student in the School of Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I work with Professor Kris Hauser in the Intelligent Motion Lab (IML) on open-world teleoperation.

Before that, I graduated with a BS in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from Washington University in St. Louis, where I worked on hardware security with Professor Xuan Zhang.
