Patrick Naughton

I'm a 4th year PhD student in the Computer Science Department at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I work with Professor Kris Hauser in the Intelligent Motion Lab (IML) on open-world robotic manipulation and teleoperation.

I'm interested in how we can design algorithms and systems that enable robots to reliably operate in unstructured environments. How can we design interfaces that enable seamless teleoperation for tasks beyond pick-and-place? How can we use data collected from these systems to automate tasks? What is the right level of control for a truly general purpose robot?

Before starting my PhD I graduated with a BS in Electrical and Systems Engineering from Washington University in St. Louis where I worked on hardware security with Professor Xuan Zhang.
